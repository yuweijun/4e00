NAME

gnome-about - The Gnome about box.  

SYNOPSIS

gnome-about

 

DESCRIPTION

gnome-about Informative little about thing that lets us brag to our friends as our name scrolls by, and lets users click to load the GNOME home pages.  

OPTIONS

No options.
